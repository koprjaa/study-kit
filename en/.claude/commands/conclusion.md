Run bonus phase B.1 per CLAUDE.md.

A conclusion is not a summary. Propose a conclusion that: moves the reader (we knew X → we know
Y), opens 1–2 perspectives, returns to the hook from the introduction, gives an explicit "so
what", and offers 3 variants of the last sentence. Write it into `study/` as the final section,
log it.
